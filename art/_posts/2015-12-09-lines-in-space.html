Lines in Space

Drawer, emulsion and milkshake straws
35.5 x 55cm

Milkshake straws have become lines creating a relationship between opposite spaces. The enveloping pool of colour offering transparency in the paint, showing both solidity within the confine of the lines and fluidity in its exploration of its boundaries.
