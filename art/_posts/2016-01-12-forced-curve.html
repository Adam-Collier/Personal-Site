Forced Curve

Tube, wheels and gloss paint on MDF
41 x 61cm

Utilising the curves of the wheels in order to push and pull the form of the paint filled tube, the syringe evokes the idea of motion in the pushing of the paint through the tube. It becomes a painting caught in a material state that will never reach the painterly expectation of drying.
